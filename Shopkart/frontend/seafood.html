Seafood Preparation Tips

Check Freshness

Fresh fish should have clear eyes, red gills, firm flesh, and a mild ocean smell. Avoid fish with cloudy eyes or strong odor.

Proper Storage

Store fresh seafood in the coldest part of your refrigerator and use within 1-2 days. For longer storage, freeze immediately.

Cooking Time

Seafood cooks quickly! Overcooking makes it tough. Fish is done when it flakes easily with a fork (about 10 mins per inch of thickness).
