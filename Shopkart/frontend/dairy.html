Health Benefits of Dairy

Dairy products are nutritional powerhouses that offer numerous health benefits:

Strong Bones

Rich in calcium and Vitamin D, essential for bone health and preventing osteoporosis.

Muscle Growth

High-quality protein in dairy helps build and repair muscle tissue.

Dental Health

Calcium and phosphorus in dairy help strengthen tooth enamel and prevent decay.

Heart Health

Potassium in dairy helps maintain healthy blood pressure levels.

Dairy Storage Tips

Refrigerate Promptly

Always store dairy products in the refrigerator at 4°C or below. Milk should be stored in its original container.

Proper Packaging

Keep cheese wrapped in parchment or wax paper, then in a plastic bag. Eggs should be stored in their carton.

Check Expiry Dates

Always check use-by dates. Consume milk within 3-5 days of opening, and eggs within 3-5 weeks of purchase.
