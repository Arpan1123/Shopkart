Meat Storage & Handling Tips

Refrigerate Immediately

Store fresh meat in the coldest part of your refrigerator (below 5°C) and use within 2-3 days.

Proper Packaging

Keep meat in its original packaging or transfer to airtight containers to prevent contamination.

Freeze for Longer Storage

For extended storage, freeze meat at -18°C. Thaw in refrigerator before use, never at room temperature.
